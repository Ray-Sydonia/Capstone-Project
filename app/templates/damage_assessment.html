Strand Test & Hair Analysis

Upload a hair strand photo — the AI model classifies its condition and generates a personalised care report

🤖
AI Hair Condition Model — hair_damage_resnet50_multilabel.keras

This module runs your trained ResNet-50 multilabel classifier. It analyses the uploaded strand image and identifies which conditions are present from the following categories: Healthy · Breakage · Chemical Damage · Dry · Heat Damage · Split Ends. A strand can belong to more than one category. The client's existing hair profile is automatically pulled in from their record — no need to re-enter it here.
